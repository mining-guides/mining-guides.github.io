Common Mistakes

Gemstones:

1 Using Efficient Miner

Efficient miner will mine extra gemstone blocks without pristine working on those extra blocks, this applies to an armadillo pet too! Disable the perk.


2 Armadillo Stat Swaps

If you have high ping or the lobby has low tps it might take time for your mining stats to update on an armadillo, if this applies to you wait 1 second after swapping to your drill.


3 Etherwarps

Without good practice or insane aim they take time to get good at. the faster you can eatherwarp the more gemstones you can break.

Mithril:

1 Low Mithril Powder

To do any sort of mithril mining you need a high efficient miner level, these methods are mithril powder heavy!

Powder Mining:

1 Pets

A mithril golem's perks to buff powder doesn't work on treasure chests and only applies when mining mithril blocks.


2 Level 171+ Mole

Mole perk is pretty much useless above level 171 only use it if you have maxed everything else.


3 Scatha Perks

While the scatha pets powder buff perk works! the wormhole perk will override your mole perk, so you use it while mining hardstone.

you shouldn't use it while powder grinding unless you can swap to it before opening chests (e.g. a fishing rod)

Gold:

1 Gold = No Money

Gold is not good for making money you do it for collection, cole weight, or a golden dragon's perks


2 Insta Mining

If you can't instantly mine gold try swapping more mineral pieces for divans armor before using your mining speed boost, buy a blue cheese

goblin omelet upgrade if you still cant insta mine
