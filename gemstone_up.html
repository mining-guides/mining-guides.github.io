Already Owned:

Already have all Fine Gemstones on your Sorrow Armor and Gemstone Gauntlet

Immediate Upgrades:

Recombobulate your Gemstone Gauntlet

Recombobulate your Sorrow

Perfect Jade Gemstones in your Sorrow and Gauntlet

Next Steps:

Buy Jaded, Recombobulated Divan Armor with 3 out of 5 chambers unlocked in each piece. You should have two Perfect Jades and one Perfect Topaz. Sell your Sorrow after you get this... obviously

Upgrade your Ring of Power to an Artifact of Power and put a Perfect Topaz in it

Get the final two chambers of your Divan Armor, and put Flawless Ambers in each slot

Buy a Titanium Artifact

Put a Perfect Amber into your Gemstone Gauntlet

Put two Perfect Ambers into each of your Divan pieces

Buy a Titanium Relic

Recombobulate your Artifact of Power

Put a Perfect Jade into your Artifact of Power

Put a Perfect Amber into your Artifact of Power

Buy a Titanium Drill DR-X655 with all Perfect Gemstones, Recombobulated, Fortune 4, Efficiency 5, Pristine 5, a Titanium-infused Fuel Tank, and a Sunny Side Goblin Omelete (make sure to forge the Titanium Drill DR-X655 from a Titanium Drill DR-X355 to save like 50 million coins, and only takes 1.5 minutes to forge)

Buy a Ruby-polished Drill Engine for your Titanium Drill DR-X655 (you can also forge these drill engines to save money)

Sell your Ruby-polished Drill Engine for a Sapphire-polished Drill Engine

Sell your Sapphire-polished Drill Engine for an Amber-polished Drill Engine

Buy a second drill with a Blue Cheese Goblin Omelete (this will be explained later in the video)

Buy a Quick Claw for your Bal

Get all Perfect Gemstones in your Artifact of Power

Buy 5 Polarvoid books and apply them on your drill

Buy a Gemstone Fuel Tank

Buy a Divan's Alloy and forge with your current Titanium Drill DR-X655 for a Divan's Drill (enjoy downtime while it's forging, or do something else in the game for a while)

Get Efficiency 10 on your Divan's Drill

Recombobulate all of your equipment

10 star your Vanquished Glowstone Gauntlet

Then, finally, the Perfectly-Cut Fuel Tank

Other Upgrades:

Get a Black Belt (or, if you're struggling, Gillsplash).

Get a Rift necklace

Recombobulate this equipment following the normal upgrade order.
