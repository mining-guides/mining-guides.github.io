Coal Mining Guide

Introduction

Coal mining is a profitable and efficient way to earn coins and mining experience in the magma fields. Here's how to get started:

Gear Requirements

To get started with divan mining, you'll need:

  • 3/4 divan armor with jade gemstones
  • Aspect of the Void with Ether Warp enchantment
  • Recommended powder distribution:
    • 5m Mithril powder
    • 4m Gemstone powder

Powder Distribution

Allocate your powder as follows:

  • Focus on mining speed 1 and 2 initially to instant mine coal
  • Put most of your Mithril powder into efficient miner and all Gemstone powder into mining fortune 2
  • Stop at Efficient Miner 80 and then invest the rest into Mining Fortune 1

Rates

With max setup, you can expect the following rates:

  • Around 5m collection/hr
  • 25-30m coins/hr
  • 400k mining xp per hour

How to Mine

Follow these steps for efficient divan mining:

  • Jump while mining hardstone and use Ether Warp while mining coal
  • Ensure you mine the majority of the vein while maintaining speed
  • When mining hardstone, mine straight to reach coal veins quicker
